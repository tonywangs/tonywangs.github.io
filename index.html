👋 Hi! I'm Tony, a freshman at Stanford pursuing a Bachelors in Computer Science and Political Science. I am originally from Pleasanton, California.

At Stanford, I serve as freshman council president, where I lead representatives from all 24 dorms to organize events for class of 2027. I’m also an organizer for TreeHacks, the largest collegiate hackathon in the US, and a consultant for Microsoft Azure through the Stanford Marketing Group.

Previously, I’ve conducted deep learning research at the Kundaje Lab at SAIL, Sean Wu Lab at Stanford Medical School, Liu Lab at Harvard TH Chan School of Public Health, and PRTRM Lab at UTA. I also founded the ACSEF SLB, a student-led nonprofit that has creates research programs for 100+ Title I schools. This summer, I’ll be a software engineering intern at Amazon.

In my free time, I love trying new coffee shops, writing poetry, and teaching martial arts.
